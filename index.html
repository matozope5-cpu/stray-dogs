Our Mission

Paws Rescue is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming stray and abandoned dogs in our community.

We operate a no-kill shelter where every dog receives medical care, proper nutrition, and socialization until we find them a loving forever home.

With your support, we can rescue more dogs, provide essential medical treatments, and expand our spay/neuter program to reduce the stray population.
